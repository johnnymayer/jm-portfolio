Tips for Beginners

Here are just a few tips for any beginners. Diving right into coding can be challenging, frustrating, and sometimes mind boggling. Rely on resources like the internet, Stack Exchange, and anyone who knows a thing or two about coding for help!

  1. Read. Read as much as possible. I cannot stress this enough. Reading resource materials will help you to really learn.
  2. Watch videos. There are lots of great videos available for reinforcement and supplement to reading materials. A video will often help you in getting a second chance to view a concept, and see its implementation.
  3. Ask questions. Never be afraid to ask a question. Questions are the gateway to expanded knowledge. No question should be left unasked.
